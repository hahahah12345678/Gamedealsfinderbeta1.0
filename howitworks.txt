Ultimate Game Deals & Free Giveaways Finder - User Guide
========================================================

Overview:
---------
This app helps you find the latest free game giveaways, the best game deals, and trending gaming news, all in one place. It features a simple tabbed interface with three main sections: Free Giveaways, Game Deals, and Gaming News.

How to Use:
-----------

1. Free Giveaways Tab
---------------------
- **Browse Giveaways:** See a list of current free game giveaways from various platforms.
- **Search:** Enter keywords in the search box to filter giveaways by title.
- **Platform Filter:** Use the platform dropdown to show giveaways for a specific platform (e.g., Steam, Epic Games).
- **Open Giveaway:** Double-click a row or click "Open" to visit the giveaway page in your browser.
- **Copy Link:** Select a giveaway and click "Copy Selected Link" to copy its URL to your clipboard.

2. Game Deals Tab
-----------------
- **Browse Deals:** View top game deals from multiple stores.
- **Search:** Enter a game title in the search box to filter deals.
- **Sort:** Use the "Sort by" dropdown to sort by Deal Rating, Price, or Savings.
- **View Details:** Select a deal to see more info and a game image on the right.
- **Open Deal:** Double-click a deal or click "Open" to visit the deal page.
- **Copy Link:** Select a deal and click "Copy Selected Link" to copy its URL.
- **Copy All Deals:** Click "Copy All Deals" to copy all visible deals (with links) to your clipboard.

3. Gaming News Tab
------------------
- **Browse News:** See the latest gaming news headlines.
- **News Type:** Use the dropdown to select between PC News, Fortnite, or Roblox news.
- **Refresh:** Click "Refresh" to update the news feed.
- **Read More:** Double-click a news item to open the full article in your browser.
- **Preview Summary:** Select a news item to see a summary below the list.

Tips:
-----
- The app auto-refreshes all tabs every 5 minutes.
- All features are accessible via the tabbed interface at the top.
- If you encounter errors, check your internet connection.



Enjoy finding the best game deals and news!
